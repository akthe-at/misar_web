Support Our Mission:

Save Lives with MISAR

Your donations are crucial to our mission. They enable us to equip our volunteers with essential gear, provide our teams with advanced training opportunities, and ensure the well being of our incredible canine search partners. When you donate, you directly support these efforts to save lives and enhance our emergency response capabilities.

If you're unable to donate financially, there are still valuable ways you can support Michigan Search and Rescue (MISAR):

Donate Your Time:

Volunteer as a "lost" person for canine training exercises.

Donate Training Space:

Do you have unused land or buildings? Offer it for MISAR training exercises.

Make a Financial Donation:

Every dollar helps. Michigan Search and Rescue is a 501(c)(3) Nonprofit Corporation and donations may be considered tax deductible.

Michigan Search and Rescue is a team of dedicated volunteers. Your support, whether through time, space, or financial contribution, makes significant impact on our ability to save lives and serve our community. Thank you for considering how you can support MISAR!
